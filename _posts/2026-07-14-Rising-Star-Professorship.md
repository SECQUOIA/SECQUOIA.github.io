David E. Bernal Neira has been recognized with a Rising Star Professorship by Purdue University, in recognition of his research accomplishments and emerging leadership in chemical engineering, optimization, and quantum computing.

As part of this recognition, Prof. Bernal Neira has been appointed the **Mitchel Papanicolas Family/Del Ray Glass Company Assistant Professor of Chemical Engineering** in the Davidson School of Chemical Engineering at Purdue University.

Congratulations to Prof. Bernal Neira and the entire SECQUOIA group!
